A friend of mine recently quit his job at Yahoo to start a neat new project, OpenPhoto - and it's a really cool concept. Unlike the existing photo sharing sites, OpenPhoto gives you full and absolute control of your pictures, as your photos are stored in your own storage bucket, rather than theirs.

Interested in helping Jaisen get this project off the ground and free your photos? Head over to the kickstart project page. Pledge a few bucks to help Jaisen meet his funding goals - just don't mention the sideburns! :-)
