Welcome to my new blog project - sail-2-software!

As we make the transition back to living on land after a six-month leave of absence to sail down to Mexico, I'm finding lots of things to write about. Sound and video electronics, as we furnish an apartment. New computer and mobile devices as we've lifted our three-year moratorium on upgrades. New software development languages, frameworks, and infrastructure as I start new projects at work. And of course boating and sailing will be represented as well, hence the theme and title - everything from sail-2-software...

You can read our cruising blog, about the voyage of our catamaran S/V Jane'O. At times I'm sure these blogs will intersect as technology clearly crosses over to my love of sailing and cruising. http://svjaneo.com will continue to focus on travels and stories interest directly related to our cruising life, where this blog has a much more general focus.

Enjoy, check back ofter, and please stay with me as this thing comes together!
