What a crazy news week! It's only Tuesday, and already we have:

  • AT&T buying T-Mobile. This is clearly a bad thing for everyone but AT&T, as we will see prices go up - and now there will be only a single major GSM carrier in the USA.
  • Microsoft suing Barnes&Noble, Foxconn, and Inventec for using Android. I guess the old adage is apropos here: "Those that can do, those that can't sue".
  • Apple suing Amazon for using the words "App Store". Apple said  "We've asked Amazon not to copy the App Store name because it will confuse and mislead customers", so Apple basically thinks their customers are stupid?
  • Iran claims to have built a flying saucer shaped drone called Zohal, and accompanied the press release with a picture of the drone. The picture, not surprisingly, was a fake - as a Google image search proved it was from an old Sci-Fi movie.
I'm curious to see what will happen today, tomorrow, and the rest of the week. Oh, and I'm not bothering with links to any of the stories - you can find them easily enough right here :-).
